Aid Transparency Tracker

Purpose

The purpose of the tracker is to analyse what information donors are currently publishing. It combines an automated assessment of data released through the International Aid Transparency Initiative (IATI), with manually collected data in order to understand what information donors are currently making available, and in what formats.

Methodology

Full details of how the information collected is assessed and scored is provided in the 2018 Aid Transparency Index technical paper.
